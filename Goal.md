Develop a generalized tech-policy stakeholder framework: for _any_ technology, identify every actor-type involved and assign each a differentiated bundle of **rights / liabilities / controls / influence** over that technology's governance and functioning. 

Test cases I care about: gig workers as a distinct class the platform is answerable to in specific ways; citizens as consumers of government tech with a different relation to decision-making than consumers of private tech.
